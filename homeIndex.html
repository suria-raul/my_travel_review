My 2017 year in review

Travel Blog

2017 has been a great year for me. I traveled a lot, been recognized by my supervisor, got promoted and been part of new and better team. I did a lot of great things and have tried something new and found an amazing opportunity to get back to the field that I love. Though there was a lot of struggles to achieve this things I still manage to conquer it and the most important thing is I AM STILL ALIVE. I will share some things that I have done in 2017. Scroll down.

Dingalan, Aurora also known as the batanes of the east is located at the east coast of Central Luzon. You will enjoy this silent and peacefull place due to a number of waterfalls and mountains with sweet, fresh and healthy foods. We went here on the month of September 2017

Taal Crater lake, one of natureâ€™s greatest wonders nearest to Manila, but also a not so beatiful place to be. Almost 25 minutes of boat from Talisay, Batangas to Taal Island. If you are looking forward to try mountain hiking you should try this place. July 2017

Amazing silhouettes, beautiful morning and cold breeze that's what Lucban, Quezon can offer also their famous taytay falls and pancit habhab will make you stay on this place, one of the greatest places I went. Wanting to me to go back to see the beautiful mornings everyday. May 2017.

Nestled 500 meters above sea level on the slopes of Mt. Natib, one of Bataan's highest peaks, Sinagtala is a 55 hectare property adjacent to the Bataan National Park. It is a place where one can find peace, quiet, and tranquility. Feb 2017

Enjoy super delicious foods, arts, belongingness, camaraderie and hundreds of dream catchers in the hippie, cool, tropical place of Dreamland Tagaytay. You can also buy new stuff in this unique restaurant. I strongly suggest that you should try their potato wedges.

Delight in the beauty of Italian architecture at the country's most romantic mall. Enjoy unique dining attractions and shopping experience. Create memorable moments at Venice Grand Canal. Feb 2017
